The animals also influence my values I learnt to value freedom form birds , from dogs I learnt stubbornness can sometimes be a very useful in getting what you want but also know when to drop it even if its the only thing you want in the world at that moment, and the value of tolerance from cats the put up with something up until the don't and you can usually see that last part coming from a good while away.
